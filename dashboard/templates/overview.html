Each metric explained:
Resolution Rate: % of complaints that have been closed (not still in-progress). Higher = better at resolving issues.
Monetary Relief: % of complaints where the bank provided financial compensation. Higher = more consumer-friendly.
Timely Response: % of complaints where the bank responded within the regulatory deadline. 100% = perfect compliance.
Health Score: Weighted composite: Resolution(30%) + Monetary Relief scaled to 100 at 20%(20%) + Timely(15%) + Trend direction(20%) + Inverse severity(15%).
Grade: A=80+ B=65-79 C=50-64 D=35-49 F=below 35
