Bank Performance Comparison ❓ How are grades calculated?
Health Grade Methodology:
Each bank receives a composite score (0-100) based on:
Resolution Rate (30%) - % of complaints fully closed (not in-progress)
Monetary Relief (20%) - % of complaints resulting in financial compensation, scaled so 20% relief = 100 score
Timely Response (15%) - % of complaints answered within regulatory timeframe
Trend Direction (20%) - Are monthly complaints increasing (bad) or decreasing (good)?
Complaint Severity (15%) - Inverse of narrative rate (more narratives = more severe complaints)
Grades: A = 80+ | B = 65-79 | C = 50-64 | D = 35-49 | F = below 35
